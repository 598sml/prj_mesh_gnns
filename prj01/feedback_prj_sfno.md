**1. My understanding**  
This project is about using Spherical Fourier Neural Operators to model fluid flow on the sphere, with the shallow water equations as a test problem.

**2. One strength**  
The project has a clear technical direction because it is based on a specific paper and a concrete benchmark problem.

**3. One concern**  
The scope still seems quite broad, especially if it includes theory, implementation, reproduction, tuning, and comparison with other models.

**4. One suggestion**  
I would suggest making reproduction of one result from the Bonev et al. paper the main goal, and treating comparisons or stretch goals as optional.
